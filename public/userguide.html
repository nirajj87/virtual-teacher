Getting Started

1

Welcome to Virtual Teacher App

This application helps you prepare for technical interviews by providing a realistic practice environment. You can answer questions, get instant feedback, and track your progress over time.

2

What You'll Learn

Master technical interview questions across various categories including Data Structures, Algorithms, System Design, Database Management, and more. The app simulates real interview conditions.

Initial Setup

Theme Selection

Toggle between Dark Mode and Light Mode using the switch in the top-right corner.

Default Questions

The app comes with a default set of technical interview questions ready to use.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the similarity scoring work?
The app uses AI-powered text comparison that considers meaning, context, and synonyms.
Can I use my own questions?
Yes! Upload CSV files with your questions. Format: Question,Answer,Category.
How is my progress saved?
Your scores and statistics are automatically saved in your browser's local storage.
